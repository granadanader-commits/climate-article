Rising Climate and Land Pressure

Welcome to the article...

Climate change refers to long-term shifts in global temperatures and weather patterns, largely driven by human activities such as burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial emissions that increase greenhouse gases in the atmosphere. These causes intensify global warming, leading to consequences such as rising sea levels. Solutions include transitioning to renewable energy, protecting forests, adopting sustainable agriculture, and strengthening international cooperation. In conclusion, addressing climate change is urgent and requires collective action to safeguard ecosystems and ensure a livable planet for future generations.
